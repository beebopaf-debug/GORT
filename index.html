Tour overview

A Private Great Ocean Road Tour Planned Around Your Day

A Great Ocean Road day trip is one of Victoria's most memorable coastal experiences, but it is also a long day from Melbourne. The distance, winding roads, changing weather, daylight, and number of possible stops all make good planning important.

This site helps you understand what is realistic in one day and choose a private tour that suits your group. Whether you want a relaxed scenic drive, a Twelve Apostles-focused day, or a more flexible custom itinerary, Australian Journeys can help shape the day around your timing and interests.

Why private

Why Choose a Private Great Ocean Road Tour?

A private tour gives you more comfort, flexibility, and control than a large public coach tour. With Australian Journeys, the day is planned for your group only.

A private tour is especially useful if you are travelling as a couple, family, small group, cruise group, or larger private group that wants the day to feel organised without feeling rushed.

Highlights

Popular Great Ocean Road Highlights

The Great Ocean Road has more worthwhile stops than most visitors can comfortably see in a single day from Melbourne. A private tour helps you focus on the places that matter most.

Twelve Apostles

The most famous viewpoint on the Great Ocean Road and a major reason many visitors choose the journey.

Loch Ard Gorge

A powerful coastal stop with cliffs, beach views, and one of the region's best-known shipwreck stories.

Rainforest and Wildlife Stops

Known wildlife areas and short rainforest walks can be included when timing and conditions allow.

Custom planning

Need a More Flexible Itinerary?

Not every group wants the same Great Ocean Road day. Some visitors want more time at the Twelve Apostles. Others prefer rainforest, wildlife areas, photography stops, a slower lunch, or fewer stops with more breathing room.

A custom Great Ocean Road itinerary helps you choose what matters most and avoid trying to fit too much into one day.
