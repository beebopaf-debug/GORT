What Is Realistic in One Day from Melbourne?

The Great Ocean Road can be done in a day from Melbourne, but it is a long day and needs prioritisation. Some stops are essential for first-time visitors; others are better suited to slower or extended trips.

Australian Journeys will give honest advice about what fits comfortably for your group.
